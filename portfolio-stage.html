The Syracuse Stage

My Public Relations Research class took on the Syracuse Stage, a local theatre company, as a client for the semester. The Stage was hit hard by the COVID-19 pandemic, and was struggling to bounce back once live theatre opened again. Throughout the semester, I worked with a team of five other students to research their audience and come up with suggestions on how the Stage can regain its audience.
