Hey, I'm Kendall!

Welcome to my portfolio website

I am a junior studying Public Relations at Syracuse University minoring in Information Management Technology and Sociology. Within strategic communications, I am interested in the diversity of digital platforms. I am fascinated by the ways that social media and technology intersect, and I love finding new ways that communications professionals can take advantage of emerging technologies. I offer strong skills in research, design and writing; being well-versed in AP style and the Adobe Creative Suite.

I am very involved in extracurricular activities. I have been involved with SU Danceworks, the largest student-run and auditions based dance organization on campus, since I came to school. From general cast member to graphics chair, to producer and now director, this organization has been instrumental to my growth as a creative and leader. I have also been involved in Hill Communications, SU's only student-run public relations firm. These past six semesters, I have been able to work with clients in a wide range of industries ranging from healthcare to education. This website showcases the work I have done inside and outside the classroom; I hope you enjoy!
