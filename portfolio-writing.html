Writing Samples

Strong writing skills are essential to any good communicator. Crafting stories that make your clients stand out amongst the crowd requires writing that is clear and concise, but also conveys the personality of the organization. During the past two years at university, I have learned how to be a flexible writer by adopting different tones to effectively reach my audience. In the Spring of 2023, I took a course called Public Relations Writing: Media and Messaging. The course has allowed me to build a vast portfolio of writing samples that reflect my skills as a strategic storyteller. At the beginning of the semester, I chose one company that I write several pieces for, as if I were the public relation manager. * The following information is not intended to be factual but is part of a class writing assignment from January to April 2023 the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, Syracuse University. All information is intended to be sourced appropriately. *
