SU Danceworks

SU Danceworks is the largest student-run and audition-based dance organization at Syracuse University. As the graphic designer for Danceworks, I was responsible for designing all visual elements related to the organization and the annual showcase in the spring. This role gave me the opportunity to strengthen my design skills and become more involved with Danceworks.

Branding

At the beginning of the year, I was tasked with creating an official brand guideline for Danceworks, as we wanted to update our branding to reflect the program's growth. I created a basic wordmark to use for birthday posts on the Danceworks Instagram page. In preparation for our spring showcase, I created an additional set of brand guidelines for our spring showcase, Danceworks VOGUE, and made the logo for that as well, inspired by the Vogue Magazine logo.

Linktree Resources

As the graphic designer, I was also responsible for managing a Linktree page for easy access to Danceworks resources throughout the year. The initial Linktree included links to an about page, photo gallery, and links to old shows. After the initial launch, I would update the links as necessary to highlight events throughout the year.

Danceworks Vogue Promotional Content

In the months leading up to our spring showcase, I worked closely with the Danceworks public relations chairs to produce content for Instagram. A month before the show, we began posting choreographer highlights on Instagram that showed snippets of the show dances. We also planned and facilitated a cast-wide photoshoot to get more content for the Instagram feed. Lastly, we planned and filmed a 45-second promotional video for the show that highlighted dancers in the cast and got people excited about the show.
