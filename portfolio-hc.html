Hill Communications

Hill Communications is Syracuse University's only student-run boutique public relations firm. Additionally, it is one of a few student-run firms that are affiliated with the Public Relations Society of America. I have been part of HillComm since my second semester at Syracuse, and the organization has given me many opportunities to put what I learn in class to practice. From education to healthcare, I have gained experience in a wide range of industries.

Hatfield Schwartz Law Group

Hatfield Schwartz Law Group is a law firm in Cedar Knolls, New Jersey that specializes in workplace and employment law. As an account supervisor, I served as a liaison between the client and the team that I led. During the course of our contract, I worked with my team to develop a social media strategy for H&S Linkedin. Our team also started an e-newsletter for them; writing the newsletters and optimizing their database of over 1,000+ clients.
