Design samples

An important part of communicating any concept is making sure that it visually captures the audience's attention. While art directors and graphic designers are usually the ones who bring these visions to life, having a solid understanding of design can help you communicate your vision to designers. I believe that a strong relationship between strategy and design will strengthen any campaign. While design isn't ultimately what I wish to pursue, I am a creative person at my core and enjoy making my work as visually appealing as possible. Throughout college and beyond, I hope to strengthen my design skills through coursework and extracurriculars.
